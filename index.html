University of Asia Pacific

University of Asia Pacific (UAP) was established in 1996 with a vision to enhance the opportunity for higher
education in Bangladesh. The University, under the Private University Act 1992, started its operation in 1996 by
offering four-year Bachelor Degree Program in Computer Science & Technology and Business Administration
only. Now the UAP offers undergraduate program in four disciplines. Its curricula have been approved by
the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Government of Peopleâ€™s Republic of Bangladesh.
